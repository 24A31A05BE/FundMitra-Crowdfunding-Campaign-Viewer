About FundMitra

New here? Let’s answer some common questions to help you understand how FundMitra works.

What is FundMitra? ⌄
FundMitra is a crowdfunding platform that connects people who need financial support with those who are willing to contribute. Whether it’s a social cause, personal need, or innovative idea, FundMitra helps bring communities together to turn ideas into reality through collective funding.
How does crowdfunding work? ⌄
Crowdfunding works by collecting small contributions from a large number of people. A campaign creator sets a funding goal, explains their purpose, and shares it with others. Supporters can then contribute any amount, and together these contributions help achieve the campaign goal.
How can I trust the campaigns? ⌄
FundMitra promotes transparency by encouraging detailed campaign descriptions, regular updates, and visible progress tracking. Users can review campaign goals, backer counts, and creator information before making any contribution, helping them make informed decisions.
Is my payment secure? ⌄
Yes, all transactions on FundMitra are processed through secure payment systems. Your financial information is protected and not stored on the platform, ensuring a safe and reliable contribution experience.
Can anyone start a campaign? ⌄
Yes, anyone with a genuine cause, idea, or project can start a campaign on FundMitra. However, users are expected to provide accurate and honest information to maintain trust and credibility within the community.
Can I donate without signing up? ⌄
Yes, you can contribute without creating an account. You only need to provide a valid contact number so that we can reach you in case of updates, refunds, or important communication regarding your contribution.
How are campaigns verified and can they be trusted? ⌄
All campaigns go through a verification process before funds are released. Our team reviews the details and supporting information provided by the campaign creator. If any campaign is found to be fraudulent, contributors are notified immediately and can choose to receive a refund or support another campaign.
Does the money go directly to the campaign creator? ⌄
No, the funds are securely held by FundMitra during the campaign period. Once the campaign ends and is successfully verified, the collected amount is released to the campaign creator. This ensures transparency and protects contributors from misuse.
